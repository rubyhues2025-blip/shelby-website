Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept commissions?

Yes! I love creating custom pieces. Commission timelines typically range from 4-8 weeks depending on size and complexity. Please contact me with your ideas and I'll provide a detailed quote.

Are your paintings for sale?

Most of my gallery pieces are available for purchase. Prices vary based on size, medium, and complexity. Contact me for current availability and pricing information.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, I can arrange international shipping for my artwork. Shipping costs and insurance will be calculated based on the destination and piece size.

Can I visit your studio?

I offer studio visits by appointment for serious collectors and those interested in commissioning work. Please reach out to schedule a visit.

Do you teach art classes?

I occasionally offer workshops and private lessons. Follow my social media or sign up for my newsletter to stay updated on upcoming opportunities.

How do you handle payment?

For purchases and commissions, I typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the balance due upon completion. I accept various payment methods including bank transfers and major credit cards.
